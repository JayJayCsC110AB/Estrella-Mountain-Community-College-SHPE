Overview

Mechanical engineering involves designing and developing mechanical systems and machines. Engineers work on everything from consumer products and vehicles to industrial machinery and power generation systems.

Roadmap

  • Foundations: Calculus, physics, materials science, programming
  • Core: Statics, dynamics, thermodynamics, materials engineering
  • Advanced: Design, manufacturing, capstone projects

Subfields

Thermal & Fluids Engineering

Study of heat transfer, thermodynamics, and fluid flow in mechanical systems. Engineers design efficient cooling systems, engines, and HVAC equipment for buildings and vehicles.

Top Companies:

Carrier Global, Danfoss, Parker Hannifin, GE Power

Mechanical Design

Development and optimization of mechanical components and systems using CAD software and simulation tools. Mechanical designers create products from consumer goods to industrial machinery.

Top Companies:

Boston Engineering, PTC, Autodesk, Siemens PLM

Manufacturing Engineering

Design and optimization of production processes including machining, assembly, and quality control. Manufacturing engineers improve efficiency, reduce waste, and ensure product quality.

Robotics & Automation

Development of robotic systems and automated machinery for industrial and consumer applications. Robotics engineers design systems for manufacturing, healthcare, and exploration.

Top Companies:

KUKA, ABB Robotics, Boston Dynamics, Universal Robots

HVAC Systems

Design and control of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems for buildings and vehicles. HVAC engineers ensure comfort while optimizing energy efficiency and indoor air quality.

Top Companies:

Carrier, Trane Technologies, Lennox International, Daikin Industries

Automotive Engineering

Design and development of vehicles including powertrains, chassis, and safety systems. Automotive engineers work on improving fuel efficiency, performance, and developing electric and autonomous vehicles.

Top Companies:

Tesla, General Motors, Ford, BMW, Toyota, Volkswagen
