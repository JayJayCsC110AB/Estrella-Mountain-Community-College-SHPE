Overview

Materials engineering focuses on discovering and developing new materials with improved properties. Engineers select, process, and test materials for applications in electronics, aerospace, energy, and manufacturing.

Roadmap

  • Foundations: Chemistry, calculus, physics, lab techniques
  • Core: Materials structure, thermodynamics, characterization, processing
  • Advanced: Materials design, testing, capstone projects

Subfields

Metals & Alloys

Development and testing of metallic materials for structural and functional applications. Materials engineers optimize alloy compositions and processing methods to enhance strength, durability, and performance.

Top Companies:

Nippon Steel, ArcelorMittal, Novelis Inc., Constellium

Polymers & Composites

Design of plastic materials and fiber-reinforced composites for lightweight, high-strength applications. These materials are widely used in automotive, aerospace, and consumer products industries.

Top Companies:

BASF, Dow Chemical, LyondellBasell, Hexcel Corporation

Ceramics & Glass

Development of ceramic and glass materials for high-temperature, optical, and electronic applications. Engineers work on improving thermal properties and creating advanced ceramic coatings.

Top Companies:

Corning Inc., Saint-Gobain, NGK Insulators, CoorsTek

Semiconductors

Production and characterization of semiconductor materials for electronic devices and integrated circuits. Semiconductor engineers work on advancing chip performance and miniaturization.

Top Companies:

TSMC, Samsung Electronics, Intel, SK Hynix, Micron Technology

Materials Testing & Analysis

Characterization and quality assurance of material properties through mechanical, thermal, and chemical testing. Materials testing ensures products meet specifications and safety standards.

Top Companies:

Instron, MTS Systems, Zwick/Roell, Shimadzu Scientific Instruments

Nanotechnology

Engineering of materials and devices at the molecular and atomic scale to create new properties and functionalities. Nanotechnology enables advances in electronics, medicine, and energy storage.

Top Companies:

Nanocomp Technologies, Unidym, Arrowhead Research, Applied Nanotech
