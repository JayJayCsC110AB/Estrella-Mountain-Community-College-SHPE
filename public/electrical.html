Overview

Electrical engineering involves designing and developing electrical equipment and systems. Engineers work on everything from power generation and distribution to electronics, telecommunications, and renewable energy.

Roadmap

  • Foundations: Calculus, physics, programming, circuit theory
  • Core: Circuits, signals, electronics, electromagnetics
  • Advanced: Power systems, controls, capstone projects

Subfields

Power Systems

Design and operation of electrical grids, power generation, transmission, and distribution systems. Power engineers ensure reliable electricity delivery while optimizing efficiency and integrating renewable energy sources.

Top Companies:

Duke Energy, NextEra Energy, Southern Company, American Electric Power

Electronics & Microelectronics

Development of semiconductors, microprocessors, and electronic devices for computing and communication. Electronics engineers design circuits and chip architectures that power modern technology.

Top Companies:

Intel, NVIDIA, AMD, Qualcomm, Texas Instruments

Telecommunications

Design and maintenance of communication networks including wireless, fiber optic, and satellite systems. Telecom engineers develop technologies for fast, reliable data transmission globally.

Top Companies:

AT&T, Verizon, Cisco Systems, Nokia, Ericsson

Control Systems

Development of automation and feedback control systems for industrial processes and applications. Control engineers design systems that monitor and adjust operations for optimal performance and safety.

Top Companies:

Siemens, ABB, Emerson Electric, Rockwell Automation, Honeywell

Renewable Energy

Design of solar panels, wind turbines, battery systems, and other clean energy technologies. Renewable energy engineers work on sustainable power solutions to meet global energy demands.

Top Companies:

NextEra Energy, First Solar, Vestas, Siemens Gamesa, Tesla Energy

Embedded Systems

Development of dedicated computer systems embedded in devices from IoT sensors to automotive controls. Embedded engineers create efficient, reliable systems with limited computing resources.

Top Companies:

Texas Instruments, STMicroelectronics, NXP Semiconductors, Arduino, Raspberry Pi
