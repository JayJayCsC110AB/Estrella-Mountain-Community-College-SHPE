Roadmap

The required classes to graduate should align with your goal. If you want to transfer to another college, you should look at their requiremnet or decide to get your associates first. Different universities have differnet requirements. For example UofA combines the electrical and computer engineering major, so you will need to complete more classes like CSC240 and MAT225,

Major Intensive

  • MAT: MAT220 -> MAT230 -> MAT240 - MAT276
  • PHY: PHY121 -> PHY131 -> PHY141
  • ECE: ECE102 -> ECE103
  • CSC: CSC110 -> CSC120 -> CSC230
  • EEE: EEE202 -> EEE203

Non Major Intensive

  • COM: COM110
  • FYE: FYE103
  • ENG: ENG101 -> ENG102
  • HU: HU -> HU
  • CRE: CRE101
  • SB: SB
  • L: L
